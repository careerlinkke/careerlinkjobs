How CareerLink Works

Your journey to employment starts here. Whether you're a job seeker or employer, here's how CareerLink connects opportunity with talent.

For Job Seekers

1. Register Your Account

Sign up with your details and create a complete job seeker profile to start applying.

2. Browse Available Jobs

Search through hundreds of verified job posts in your industry and location.

3. Apply & Pay Application Fee

Choose between Direct Placement (KSh 950) or Interview Booking (KSh 350) and submit your application.

Get Started

For Employers

1. Submit a Job Post

List a job with full description and pay KSh 550 for premium placement & faster pairing.

2. Review Candidates

Access shortlisted, vetted applicants and communicate directly with them on the platform.

3. Hire & Rate

Hire the perfect match and rate your hiring experience to improve our services.
