About CareerLink

CareerLink is a recruitment platform designed to connect job seekers in Kenya with real employers, with a focus on Nairobi, Mombasa, Nakuru, and Kisumu.

We offer affordable and accessible job placements, both direct and interview-based, with detailed applications and payment confirmation. Our mission is to make job hunting fast, fair, and stress-free.

We prioritize verified listings, transparency, and human support throughout the process. Join thousands of successful applicants on CareerLink today.
