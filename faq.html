Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers. Here are some of the most common queries we receive from job seekers and employers.

Job Seeker Questions

1. How do I apply for a job?

To apply, sign in to your account, browse jobs, and click "Apply". Complete your details and follow the payment step if required.

2. What is Direct Placement vs Interview Booking?

Direct Placement (KSh 950) guarantees you a job placement. Interview Booking (KSh 350) schedules you for an interview and prepares you for success.

3. Why do I need to pay to apply?

The small fee filters out unserious applicants and supports faster placement, preparation, and employer coordination.

4. Can I get a refund?

Refunds are only applicable if a job is canceled or not available after payment. Contact our support within 24 hours.

Employer Questions

1. How do I post a job?

Create an employer account and fill in the job details. Premium listings cost KSh 550 and appear as featured jobs.

2. How long will my job stay live?

Your job post will remain live for 21 days unless manually removed. Featured jobs are pinned on the homepage for 7 days.

3. Can I hire candidates directly?

Yes, you can contact shortlisted candidates directly from your dashboard once they apply.
