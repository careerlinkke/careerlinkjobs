Privacy Policy

At CareerLink, we are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your personal information with transparency and care. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, store, and protect your data.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal details (name, email, phone number) when signing up.
  • Job application data including resume, cover letter, and preferences.
  • Browsing behavior and interaction history on our site.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To match you with relevant job opportunities.
  • To enable communication with employers.
  • To improve our services and provide personalized content.
  • To process payments for premium services.

3. Data Storage & Security

Your information is securely stored in Firebase. We implement strong encryption and access controls to protect your data from unauthorized access or disclosure.

4. Sharing Your Information

We only share your data with trusted employers you apply to, or when legally required. We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

5. Cookies

We use cookies to enhance your browsing experience and remember your login sessions. You can disable cookies in your browser settings.

6. Your Rights

  • You may access, update, or delete your account at any time.
  • You may request data export or erasure by contacting our support.

7. Updates to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any significant changes will be communicated via email or notification on your dashboard.

If you have any questions regarding this policy, please contact us.
